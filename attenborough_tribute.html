"This film is my witness statement."

Sir David Attenborough

Sir David Attenborough standing in front of a waterfall.

Sir David Attenborough has long been the most recognizable advocates for all nature and wildlife around the world for decades. His documentaries have shown millions of people so much of the ecosystems across the planet, from the frigid polar regions to the teeming life of the rainforests. In his most recent documentary he has shown the incredibly destructive effect mankind has had on wildlife diversity and how we have contributed to climate change over the time he has been filming nature on Earth.

Here are a selection of some of his documentaries from his time beginning as a broadcaster in the 1950s.

  • Quest for the paradise birds, 1957.
  • The world about us, 1967.
  • Life on Earth, 1979.
  • The living planet, 1984.
  • The first Eden, 1987.
  • Life in the freezer, 1993.
  • The life of birds, 1998.
  • The blue planet, 2001.
  • Planet Earth, 2005.
  • Frozen planet, 2011.
  • Planet Earth II, 2016.
